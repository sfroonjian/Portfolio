Hawaii Vacation Weather

Github Repository

Everyone hopes to have great weather when they go on vacation, but it can be hard to plan a vacation in advanced when you are unsure what the weather is going to be like. The purpose of this app is to create APIs that track the precipitation and temperature of 9 locations in Hawaii over the past few years. Users can enter the dates they are planning on going on vacation into a URL to see what the precipitation and temperatures of that date range were in previous years in order to predict the best time for them to go on their trip.

This was created using Python, SQLAlchemy, Pandas, and Matplotlib to extract, query, and manipulate data from SQLite, then using Flask to render the data from the database to webpage routes.
